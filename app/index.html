ABOUT THE SCHOOL, ABOUT SURFING, ABOUT THE CANARIANS

It is no coincidence that the Canary Islands are called European Hawaii - this is perfect place for fans surf: stable warm wind all year round, sandy beaches, kilometers of unspoiled expanses and clear sea. A real mecca for surfers becomes Fuerteventura - the second largest island of the archipelago.

It is here that the best place in Spain where you can study surfing. Besides that in winter and fall here huge Atlantic waves come, Fuerteventura boasts many shallow water calm lagoons, excellent suitable for teaching beginner surfers.

Surfing in the Canary Islands is available all year round - both waves and the right weather is always there. And the wave suitable for the level, can be found by both a real pro and a beginner. It is no coincidence that it is believed that exactly this place is the best suitable for learning here it is possible to move to a new level without difficulties.

Listing the advantages, one cannot get around the fact that the beaches of Fuerteventura the longest in the Canaries. IN ultimately impossible imagine them crowded with tourists. You will only encounter those who, like you, will interested in one goal - surfing training.

So far, you only want to understand what surfing is, or have already experienced all the charm of riding the waves, but you want to raise your level? Our surf school will be able to offer training for any level of experience.

Four hours of exciting activities a day will help you confidently conquer waves, feel real surfer.

The main advantages of our Russian school are high quality teaching and organization of classes, security (all our clients are insured by Allianz), excellent inventory and equipment (provided on the whole curriculum), including including soft safety boards for beginners.

First we determine the level of skating and physical fitness and only then make a program preparation. And we can to assure that after the training is over, you will not only receive a lot of great sensations, but this level rise to a new level.

A huge advantage of our school is its own teaching methodology. We have developed own learning style surfing, which allows beginners to get on the board as quickly as possible.

It was our methodology and our teachers that allowed us to pass the certification of the Surfing Federation Canary Islands - FEDERACIÓN CANARIA DE SURF. CONQUER THE TOP

The teachers of our surf school are among the best surfers in the Canary Islands. Among them - European champion in longboard, who will gladly share his experience with both beginners and more experienced surfers. All members of the teaching staff are certified members of the Surfing Federation Canary Islands. Some of them know Russian, which definitely positions us as a Russian school surfing abroad!

The entire coaching staff regularly participates in seminars and refresher courses, which held all over the world various famous surf clubs and federations. Therefore, in our surf school you can be calm: you are being taught the best trainers!

The method of learning to surf is designed for both beginners, intermediate level surfers, and TOP level surfers. Coaches find an approach to everyone, despite the fact that learning to surf at our school produced in groups. However, we we offer personal surf lessons - just you and the coach!
